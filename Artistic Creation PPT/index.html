Artistic Creation PPT

PART 1 : Original Design

I like watching anime. Last year, I watched an anime called Suzume no Tojimari by Shinkai Makoto. The movie was about a girl, a chair, a cat and a door. Simultaneously, I selected the woodworking elective as one of the electives and the teacher asked us to choose our own project. I thought that the chair in the anime would work. Therefore, I started working on it.

PART 2 : Work Start

In woodworking class, the teacher told us to make a project by ourselves. The image of Souta popped into my head. I decided to make the chair piece by piece from the first step.

This is the sketch I made about the chair.

This is the making process.


PART 3 : APP used

After I came home, I hope I could give life to my chair and give it music. Therefore, I used 4 apps to build a video about my chair. They are 3ds max, garage band, adobe after effects and adobe premiere pro.

I have been interested in building 3d models from a long time ago. Therefore, I started learning about how to make 3d models. Soon, I built my chair out of 3ds max.



I have made this chair moving just like the characters in the animation. However, the process of making this live is very complicated and I have only used the basic parts of making 3ds max. Therefore, I only got to give my chair some simple movements.

3ds max is a very complicated and professional app. There are so many modules to learn. I only got to learn the basic parts of building and using the app. I will learn more about how to use this app.

Part 4 : Making Music

I also made a piece of music using garage band. I have been using this app since I was in school and I can make sheet music from tunes. I listened to the music from Suzume and wrote it into a violin + viola version by myself. However, this is not as professional as the other three apps.

I use viola and violin separately to make two soundtracks. This is a picture I drew of myself recording trhe soundtracks.

After that, I added my model and music together into a video using adobe after effects and adobe premiere pro.
